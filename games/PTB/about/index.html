About

I. Game Rules

The game is very simple. Just click as many buttons as you can before the time runs out.

II. Settings

You can choose to play within 60, 30, or 10 seconds.

III. Trivia

  1. The game was only used for testing purposes and was quickly discontinued after assuring the Unity version was compatible.
  2. The game icon is the default Unity build icon.
