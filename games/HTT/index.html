Inspiration

I saw that my classmates’ willingness to recite English vocabularies is very low, and they admitted that if memorising vocabularies is a game, then they must fight for the first place. This prompted me to create a game that helps doing so. To my surprise, the project received enthusiastic responses from my classmates. There were unexpectedly many people who were willing to participate. Luckily, the time also coincided with the period of self-learning courses. Therefore, the production of Hsueh Tse Tan officially kicked off.
