III. Collectibles

  1. Cubes

    There are 10 per level.

  2. Spheres

    There are 0 to 3 per level. Not a necessity and merely a surprise. Placed on unexpected places in the level. Players can decide whether or not to collect it; it does not affect the final score.

IV. Light

Players can light up lights for every level.

  1. Blue Light

    Complete at least 50%.

  2. Orange Light

    Pass the level and collect all cubes without enabling guidance lines.

  3. Purple Light

    The condition of Orange Light plus collecting every spheres. Since not all levels have spheres, Purple Light may be impossible for some levels.

V. Locked Features

Some special features are locked until the criteria is met.

  1. Guidance Lines

    Shows you da wae. Available for all levels. To enable, at least pass the first checkpoint.

  2. 2D Mode

    Birdwatching view. Everything is flat. Note that levels are not made specifically for 2D modes, so expect unexpected behaviours. Available to all levels. To enable, at least have the orange light lit up.

VI. Account System

The account is only used for saving game data, nothing else is recorded.

Inactive accounts will face deletion. On the 1st day of every month, an email will be sent to users who “have not logged in for at least 28 days.” On the 28th day of the same month, those said accounts will be deleted forever.

When an account is logged in, local data and cloud data will start to sync. Normally, the larger values will replace the smaller ones. Only level progresses are synced to cloud.
